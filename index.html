The ETEEAP Process

A step-by-step journey to convert your professional experience into an accredited degree.

1

Documentation & Application

Submit a comprehensive professional portfolio including Letter of Intent, detailed CV, Certificates of Employment, recommendation letters, and Prior Learning Sheet.

2

Verification & Interview

Initial verification of documents followed by an orientation interview to assess professional maturity and authenticate submitted claims.

3

Assessment & Proficiency Validation

Rigorous evaluation by a Panel of Assessors through written tests, oral defense, skills demonstration, and portfolio assessment.

4

Deficiency Mapping & Enrollment

Receive a personalized curriculum showing accredited units and remaining "Enrichment Courses" to fill knowledge gaps.

5

Final Evaluation & Degree Award

Complete supplementary courses and undergo final evaluation. ETEEAP Council recommends granting of the degree with same prestige as traditional 4-year degree.
